Goodbye Lonely Planet, Hello NSS

Today is my last day as Manager of Software Development at Lonely Planet and I will miss working with this amazing team that I helped build. I know they go on to do great things with the skills that I helped them grow over the last 1.5 years. They are my friends, not just my teammates.

Tomorrow I start as Lead Instructor for the front end development classes at Nashville Software School. I've been teaching the evening class for the past 6 months and it has been one of the most fulfilling experiences of my life. I've always wanted to be a teacher, and now that I've got a taste for it, I know now that it's what I want to be when I grow up.

Classes start on July 6 for day cohort 10, and evening cohort 2. It will be some long days on Mondays and Wednesdays, but it will be incredible at the same time.
